Project: Walmart Kiosk

The project involved using advanced formulas and macros in Excel to create a Walmart kiosk where customers could look up products in stock. A form was created in Excel where customers could enter the unique ID for a product and retrieve its details and perform brand and category analysis. In addition, the kiosk provided recommended alternatives if the product was not available.
